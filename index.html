Should you break up?

You've been going back and forth for weeks. You've googled it, rehearsed the conversation in the shower, asked your most trustworthy friend and your least trustworthy friend. You already know the answer — you're just looking for permission.

This quiz reads your answers and gives you the most honest, direct response it can. The kind a brutally honest friend would give if they had no stake in the outcome. No hedging. No "only you can decide".

Common situations: constant fighting, emotional unavailability, cheating and broken trust, feeling nothing anymore, long distance strain, controlling behaviour, growing apart.

Signs people describe before asking

often means leave
  • You dread going home
  • More alone with them than without
  • You've had the same fight 200 times
  • Your friends have quietly stopped asking about them
  • You're staying because of history, not now
worth working through
  • You fight but you repair — genuinely
  • It's a rough patch with a clear cause
  • You still like each other as people
  • Scared of change, not scared of them
  • You've grown together, not just grown
genuinely complicated
  • Long distance with no end date
  • Mental health is a factor
  • You love them but aren't in love
  • Perfect relationship, wrong timing
  • You know what to do. You're not ready.
